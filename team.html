I work as a Lab Manager at the Enkavi Lab at Claremont McKenna College. I am from Turkey, and I hope to use my CS degree to better understand the brain. I previously founded and captained the All-Gender Volleyball Club at the Claremont Colleges, worked in career counseling, led the Turkish Student Association, and mentored for the International Student Mentorship Group. Off work hours, you’ll find me biking, playing volleyball, or reading a book on a piece of grass anywhere around Claremont.
